LEN IS BACK. SUMMER STARTS NOW.

MUSIC Jun 2026

do you want to know my pet peeve? this ridiculous narrative that the underground scene "died off" because artists are taking longer to drop now. did you lot never learn that patience is a virtue? good things come to those who wait. well, the waiting is over. len is gearing up. couldn't come at a better time either. sun is shining. the people are outside. eyes and ears are open. the time is right.

i've been listening to len since may 2022. I actually remember the moment. in the stockroom at work. trying to find a customer a size 10 pair of jordan 4 fire red's. the best 4, while we're on the subject. i'm procrastinating. on bullshit. i open spotify, and see a song in my discover weekly called brandnewbih. i play it. yeah. instant fan. sonically, totally unique. song sounds like vapour. like kaa from the jungle book. smooth. that was the song of the summer for me that year. 2023 was addiction. 2024 was tellmesum. 2025 was all me. what a run.

which brings us nicely to 2026. snippets and visuals are flying around. backstage. goldenboy listening party in paris. music got me grooving differently. got me strutting through the streets. confidence is elevating. len has never been afraid to change his sound. anyone who's been listening to him for a minute knows that. but this has a feel. m.i.a sample. the clubs are gonna like this one. music to make you want to buy some mui mui and cut your talking stage off. i'm here for it. the uk is hungry for goldenboy. i have a feeling we're about to be fed.

nice one.
