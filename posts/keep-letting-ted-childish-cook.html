PLEASE KEEP LETTING TED CHILDISH COOK

CULTURE Jul 2026

you know where i associate the centre of the creative world? old street station. not because it has any significance to me as a place, other than that its renovations confused me every time i stepped out of it. for me, it was the starting point for so many of my most cherished experiences. i walked from there to xoyo to cover my first concert as a journalist, and, a year later, to shoot homixide gang. a year on from that, i walked from there with my friend diego to watch yt at colours hoxton. eight months on from that, i was exiting the station again. this time, it was at the behest of ted childish.

look, even if i wasn't slightly biased from having been a part of yapsule, a wonderful event that brought some of the uk's greatest creatives together, ted would still be one of my all time favourites. my introduction to him and his work came when he designed brockhampton's coachella merch. 2022. the break up. probably my favourite band of all time. scenes. from there, ted has worked with the lot. feels superfluous to just list off names, but he's worked with the likes of stussy, badbadnotgood, ice cream, and seth rogen. strong, is that. not to be sniffed at.

ted's work is far more than who he's worked with. you need only take a look at his most infamous exploits. for one, there was the frank prank (the frank, if you will), that took the internet by storm. this man had everyone and their dog believing frank ocean was coming back. had mates showing me it and asking if it was real. as we know now, it was an elaborate stunt from the mind of ted childish. but why let that stop their fun? today, he's a resident at manor place, alongside a plethora of other talented folk. great company. he recently headed up creative for kareem's keep the meter running. loving it. need ted's subway take though. get the comments riled up. ready for a scrap. a class creative. go have a nosey. what a way to spend an evening.

nice one.
