3STACKS: THEIR TIME IS UP, YOUR TIME IS NOW

CULTURE Jun 2026

3stacks. their time is up, your time is now. we see you. been thinking about her modelling england's world cup collection with palace and really had to sit back and take in her body of work.

first got put on to her in may 2023. an event in bristol. lua and kehn9 djing to a fairly small crowd. imagine you tried booking that now.

three years on, and stacks has built a formidable career. visual director. filmmaker. an essential cog in this swiss-watch like music scene we're all experiencing. gaff would be rotating off its axis without her.

stacks has been a vital part of some of the most infamous shows in recent history. esdee. finessekid. sinn6r. touring with lexa gates. curated her own victory lap set. one of my favourite lineups too. ceesho and yt on the same set? i'll have some of that.

today, stacks is a resident at manor place. world's her oyster. tony montana job. take in kino studios. take in her direction. a true artist, is 3stacks. if i had a hat, i would tip it.
