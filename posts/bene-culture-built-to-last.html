BENE CULTURE IS BUILT TO LAST

CULTURE Jul 2026

it's bizarre what we deem to be a hallmark of greatness, and when we deem it to be so. if you're a footballer, you might aspire to be the great of your position. my old man loved maldini and them man. not quite on the same level. the cwmbran cafu. decent player. he took me to london for the first time when i was 15. sightseeing. at the time, i was just starting to get into fashion, and all day long, i pestered him to take me to end. the route he'd devised eventually took us through soho, and lo and behold, we were there. that, to me, was the mountaintop. the best brands, the best store, the best staff. the lot. in some ways, i still hold it in that esteem. it's an institution. today, bene culture is at its heart and centre.

back up the m1, bene culture have asserted themselves as a force in the birmingham fashion space. with every new drop, there's a piece i want. the leopard print waffle knit. the joggers. the black crest jumper. the board shorts. i could go on. jorja smith collab back in the day too. brum's best and brightest knew it had to be done. well class.

while bene culture have been stocked in end. for a minute, this recent collaboration has a feel to it. like i mentioned earlier, there's a level of reverence attached to end. as an entity. it's not just retail. these lot are tastemakers. i remember the days when they had the aimé leon dore rainbow jumpers. red to orange to yellow and so on. i feel we'll be looking back on this collaboration with the same esteem. it's different. the track set looks straight out of the 03/04 premier league season. something blackburn rovers would be rocking on an away day. complimentary. cracking stuff. that usa 94 tee is speaking to me though. i'm in love. bene and end. it has a ring to it. i would say they've laid their foundations, but fuck all that. these lot have built the gaff, planted the flag, and asserted themselves as a force to be reckoned with. one of my favourites brands.

decent.
