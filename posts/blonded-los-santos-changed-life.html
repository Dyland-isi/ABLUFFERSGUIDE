BLONDED LOS SANTOS CHANGED MY LIFE

MUSIC Jul 2026

i think about the summer of 2017 every day. that was when my love affair with music truly started. my birthday present that year was an apple music subscription. gift card job. activated the code outside the changing rooms of matalan. had been dragged back to school shopping i imagine. i was 13. for those first few months, i remember listening to anything and everything i could get my hands on. i became a music nerd. then, in december, blonded los santos was added to grand theft auto v.

to say it was transformative for me feels like an overstatement, but it was. i, like everyone my age, played gta. i was a radio los santos man. but blonded changed the lot for me. despite my obsession with finding new music, i struggled to find artists that actually spoke to me. you can listen to all the new releases you want, but if it's not for you, it's not for you. suddenly, here was this playlist that hit every note. it had rap, r&b, soul, grime, electronic, indie, and everything in between. it formed the backbone of my music taste. nine years on, it still does.

this station did wonders for me. it was my first proper introduction to frank ocean, bar the odd song here and there. when a video game has introduced you to your favourite artist of all time, it's done something right. it taught me the importance of tone and curation. i'd enjoyed uzi's luv is rage 2, but had skipped over for real for the hits on that album. within this new context, suddenly it was uzi's best song. same goes for dead presidents. i was a jay-z fan, but surface level. i'd enjoyed 4:44, but now i was scouring his back catalogue for another hidden gem to show off. man don't care. need i say more?

what blonded did, more than anything else, was shape my taste. from these introductions to frank, aphex twin, and marvin gaye, the station continues to inform how i find and consume music. it made me curious. it taught me the importance of place, tone, and curation. lessons which remain at the forefront anytime i listen to a new artist. it still inspires me. i love it.

cheers.
