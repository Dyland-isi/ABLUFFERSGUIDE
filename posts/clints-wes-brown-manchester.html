CLINTS X WES BROWN: A MANCHESTER MASTERCLASS

CULTURE Jun 2026

clints and wes brown. never have i seen a collaboration that felt this right. you lot already know clints. one of manchester's greatest exports. the varsity puffer has been on my mind since it dropped. when was that, nearly four years ago? mad. world cup fever has us all in a chokehold, but this took me by the scruff of the neck. clear your moodboards. start over. they put wes brown in a gaffer jacket. all bets are off. i love this.

it's a masterclass in understanding your audience. sure, every brand can reference football culture. lord knows they do. the collection is class, but to build it around a cult hero like wes brown is beautiful. this isn't streets won't forget. the streets of manchester will long remember wes. two champions leagues. five premier leagues. 232 appearances. clints, you lot have outdone yourselves. come on you reds. come on clints.

ps, i met wes brown at a charity golf tournament when i was 16. sound as fuck. my old man was beside himself. bleeds united, does he. probably got his autograph knocking about in the attic somewhere. happy days.
