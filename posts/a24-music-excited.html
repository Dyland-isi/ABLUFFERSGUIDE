WHY YOU SHOULD BE EXCITED ABOUT A24 MUSIC

MUSIC Jul 2026

a24 music know ball. you should be very excited.

to the shock of (i hope) absolutely no one, i'm a film nerd. you can thank the old man for that. og bluffer. has me rooting around the blu-ray section of cex every birthday and father's day. committed. as my taste in film developed, i, like so many others, gravitated towards projects backed by a24. the odd stinker notwithstanding, they've been behind some of my favourite films of all time. aftersun. the iron claw. midsommar. the drama is my favourite film of the year so far. their filmography is ludicrously stacked. banger on banger on banger. while they've always released soundtracks and scores, i was intrigued when i heard about they had started signing artists to their own label, a24 music. curiosity gave way to intrigue, and now, intrigue has given way to excitement. here's why.

to understand why you should be so excited by a24's latest foray into music, you need only look at their first signee: mark william lewis. the london-based singer-songwriter might have seemed a slightly left-field choice to some. to me, this choice was a statement of intent. mark william lewis is a ludicrously talented artist. one of the jewels in his crown has always been his ability to craft immersive soundscapes that, while subtle, are emotionally overwhelming. tracks like 2022's pleasure is everything stop you dead. it's hypnotic. the best a24 films carry that same impact. mark william lewis couldn't fit the bill better.

a24 music's other signees hit the mark too. sophia stel's taste, girlsweetvoiced's spearmint, and ear's rumspringa will knock your socks clean off. in truth, what excites me most about this line-up is its curation. every artist feels culturally similar, yet sonically, they're on different planets. you're not going to mistake a sophia stel song for a girlsweetvoiced project. there's an implacable quality which binds them together too. when i think a24, their films have a touch of spielbergian light about them. a beautiful haze. they radiate off the screen. their artists feel the same. there's a glow. a quality so few others can match. may their lights continue to shine bright.

class.
