MURA MASA AND LEDBYHER: MATCH MADE IN HEAVEN

MUSIC Jun 2026

mura masa and ledbyher. needja. what a wonderful meeting of the minds. this means something to me. i got into music properly at 13. mura masa was one of the first artists i fell in love. love$ick had us all in a chokehold. steel drums and sun bouncing off the pavements. nowt tops it, even now. collaborations with nao were moments i cherish.

past few months, he's been working with the next generation's best and brightest. track with sophia stel is no joke. this one, though…match made in heaven. you lot already know rachy is the one. haven't gone a day without thinking about her recent headline show at the lower third. class.

this track is speaking to me. rachy's chopped up vocals and mura masa's emo-inspired production complement each other beautifully. visuals are mad too. shot on a sony ericsson. second-row kitchen drawer staple. underneath the bills, pens, and envelopes. this is nostalgia i'm here for. brilliant effort. chuck this one in your playlists. cheers.
