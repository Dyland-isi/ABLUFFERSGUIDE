NICK CHEO: FROM FRAT PARTIES TO FASHION WEEK

MUSIC Jun 2026

nick cheo. inspirational y'know. took his hobby from frat parties to fashion week. travelling the world. got the folks busting shapes. getting loose.

got his start posting dj clips on his flx-4. loads of them blew up. not without reason. curation is perhaps the most important skill a dj can have, alongside technical ability. nick knows his craft well. his mixes feel contextual. playboi carti and charli xcx should be mixed together. their music and cultural status are mirror images. each hits the same for a different fraternity. youth. sweat. the parties. having a laugh with your mates.

in truth, nick makes dj'ing look easy. streets will not forget his mixing jazz is for ordinary people with i know. made me pick up the decks myself. still learning.

in the span of just a couple of years, nick has taken clips recorded while studying in ohio to paris fashion week, on a us tour with pinkpantheress, and to stages across the globe. he's got the youth moving. feeling free. smile on his face the whole time. class existence, is that. smashing it. nick cheo, you have inspired me.
